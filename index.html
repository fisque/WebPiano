HOW IT WORKS

  • To play the piano, use the keys on your keyboard "ASDFJKL:" or click on the keys below!
  • You can record what you play by clicking the "Start Recording" button, or pressing "Spacebar".
  • Check out the "Songs To Play" tab for some play-along songs!
  • All recorded music will be in the "Recorded Music" tab.
